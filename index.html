Welcome to Qi Wang's group @ SDIAT!

Our group is a research group lead by Dr. Qi Wang at Shandong Institute of Advanced Technology (SDIAT,山东高等技术研究院), China.

Our reseach interests include phonon transport with multiple atomic simulation methods (DFT, MD, DFTB, Machine Learning... ) and fabricating aerogels with ultralow thermal conductivity.
