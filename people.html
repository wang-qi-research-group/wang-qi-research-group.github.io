Qi Wang (王琦)

Dr. Qi graduated from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2014, and obtain his PhD degree at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST, supervised by Prof. Baoling Huang) in 2019. After that he worked as postdoc at HKUST (in Prof. Baoling Huang's group) and the University of Hong Kong (in Prof. Yue Chen's group). In 2022, he joined the SDIAT, Shandong, China.
