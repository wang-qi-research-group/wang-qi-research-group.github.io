Theoretical Simulations

Phonon transport properties can be accurately predicted or calculated with first-principles-based methods (DFT, AIMD and DFTB) and phonon Boltzmann transport equation (BTE); Such a routine process has achieved great success in past years with improved description towards phonon frequency and lifetime, e.g., by considering strong anharmonicity and four-phonon scattering.

In strong anharmonic solids, coherent phonon transport is found to be important at room temperature. Wigner transport equation can be used to solve this puzzle.

We are more frequently using Machine Learning Potentials (MTP and NEP) to replace of first-principles methods for simulating complex solid systems.
